This document outlines the potential future direction for the Horse Racing Analytics AI platform. It is a living document, subject to change based on user feedback, technological advancements, and market opportunities.

## Vision Statement

To become the indispensable AI-powered co-pilot for the global horse racing community, providing the most advanced, intuitive, and actionable insights to enthusiasts and professionals at every level.

---

### Phase 1: Short-Term Goals (Next 3-6 Months)

*Focus: Enhance core experience, refine existing features, and act on immediate user feedback.*

-   **User Accounts & Preferences**:
    -   Implement persistent user accounts to save settings and preferences.
    -   Allow users to "favorite" specific tracks, horses, or jockeys for personalized alerts.
-   **Dashboard Enhancements**:
    -   Add advanced filtering and sorting options to the prediction dashboard (e.g., filter by track condition, distance).
    -   Integrate historical odds data to show trends.
-   **J.A.R.V.I.S. V2**:
    -   Improve conversational context memory.
    -   Introduce the ability for J.A.R.V.I.S. to proactively offer insights based on on-screen data.
-   **Expanded Race Coverage**:
    -   Increase the number of supported countries and tracks, focusing on Japan and the Middle East.

---

### Phase 2: Medium-Term Goals (6-12 Months)

*Focus: Expand the platform's capabilities and introduce new, high-value features.*

-   **Native Mobile Applications (iOS & Android)**:
    -   Develop a dedicated mobile app for on-the-go access, featuring push notifications for race alerts and odds changes.
-   **Historical Performance Analysis**:
    -   Build a dedicated section for analyzing past races and historical data.
    -   Allow users to back-test our AI's performance against historical results.
-   **Community & Social Features**:
    -   Introduce user profiles, leaderboards for prediction accuracy, and the ability to share analyses.
    -   Create a forum or chat section for community discussion.
-   **Advanced AI Analysis**:
    -   Incorporate real-time weather data and its impact on track conditions and horse performance.
    -   Introduce AI-driven "Pace Analysis" to predict the likely speed and running style of a race.

---

### Phase 3: Long-Term & "Blue Sky" Goals (1-3 Years)

*Focus: Revolutionize the user experience and establish the platform as an industry leader through groundbreaking innovation.*

-   **Proprietary Data Integration**:
    -   Form partnerships with stables, trainers, and data providers to access exclusive data (e.g., horse biometrics, workout data) to train a next-generation predictive model.
-   **Betting Slip Integration**:
    -   Partner with major, regulated betting platforms to allow users to seamlessly place bets based on AI insights directly within the application (subject to legal and regional compliance).
-   **Augmented Reality (AR) Race Companion**:
    -   Develop an AR feature for mobile that allows users to point their phone at a live race on a screen and see real-time data overlays, odds, and AI insights for each horse.
-   **AI-Generated Video Content**:
    -   Utilize generative video models to create dynamic pre-race analysis videos and post-race summary highlights, complete with AI-generated voiceovers.
-   **Hyper-Personalized AI Models**:
    -   For top-tier subscribers, offer the ability to train a personalized AI model that learns from their own betting history, risk tolerance, and analytical preferences to provide truly bespoke recommendations.
